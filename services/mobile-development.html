Mobile development

Developers of Android, iPad, iPhone, Blackberry, Symbian, and windows Mobile Products. Cross platform Mobile Development - Mobile Apps Fast to More Markets with Less Code!

When it comes to mobile development, look no further than iMustardSoft. With our experience, expertise and talent, we can bring your mobile app idea to downloadable life.


iMustardSoft is a pioneer in mobile application development, especially in using Ruby. We are a strategic partner with Rhomobile, a cross-platform mobile application software provider. The primary benefits of the Rhodes framework are:

The productivity and portability enabled by writing interfaces in HTML once (and compiling to native smartphone apps)

Ever wonder why mobile application development takes so long and cost so much? Now, iMustardSoft can help you to reduce it dramatically by taking advantage of the amazing productivity from writing apps in Rails and enabling it for mobile application development.

Cross-platform access to device capabilities, from a common library, used on all smartphone devices means Program once and expand to all client markets.

There are so many devices out there in the market -- Android, iPad, iPhone, Blackberry, Symbian, and Windows Mobile etc and each demands a different technology. iMustardSoft can make development and maintenance much easier by build-one-place-and-use-it anywhere philosophy - you will only need one code base to work for all platforms. For our clients that means at least 26% savings.


The ability to easily incorporate synchronized data for offline use

Wonder how to enable your users to get access to data on the run, even when they don’t have an Internet connection? iMustardSoft implements Rhosync to enable off-line access to your data, so that your users will have access to the important data wherever they are.
