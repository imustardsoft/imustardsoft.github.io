Ruby 1.9.2 gems issues and resolution

Posted by Jack on October 11, 2010 | 1 comments

Recently, we have started using ruby 1.9.2, with little problems. With the nifty tool of rvm, managing multiple version of ruby vm has never been easier. However, I had some problems with the roo, an Excel parsing gem today. The error message is something like the following:

/.rvm/gems/ruby-1.9.2-head/gems/ruby-ole-1.2.10.1/lib/ole/storage/base.rb:376:in `validate!‘: OLE2 signature is invalid (Ole::Storage::FormatError)
from /.rvm/gems/ruby-1.9.2-head/gems/ruby-ole-1.2.10.1/lib/ole/storage/base.rb:368:in `initialize’
from /.rvm/gems/ruby-1.9.2-head/gems/ruby-ole-1.2.10.1/lib/ole/storage/base.rb:110:in `new’
from /.rvm/gems/ruby-1.9.2-head/gems/ruby-ole-1.2.10.1/lib/ole/storage/base.rb:110:in `load’
from /.rvm/gems/ruby-1.9.2-head/gems/ruby-ole-1.2.10.1/lib/ole/storage/base.rb:77:in `initialize’
from /.rvm/gems/ruby-1.9.2-head/gems/ruby-ole-1.2.10.1/lib/ole/storage/base.rb:83:in `new’
from /.rvm/gems/ruby-1.9.2-head/gems/ruby-ole-1.2.10.1/lib/ole/storage/base.rb:83:in `open’
from /.rvm/gems/ruby-1.9.2-head/gems/spreadsheet-0.6.4.1/lib/spreadsheet/excel/reader.rb:1130:in `setup’

I couldn’t realy figure out what the issue is, until I switched back to ruby 1.8.7 in desperation. And guess what, it worked.

Also, a couple of gems can’t be installed for ruby 1.9.2.

it seems that 1.9.2. still has some way to go.
